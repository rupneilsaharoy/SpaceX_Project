DEVELOPMENT AND TEST FACILITY

Space X operates their Rocket Development and Test Facility in McGregor, Texas. All Space X rocket engines are tested on rocket test stands, and low-altitude VTVL flight testing of the Falcon 9 Grasshopper v1.0 and F9R Dev1 test vehicles were carried out at McGregor.

Falcon 9 v1.1 rocket cores under construction at the SpaceX Hawthorne facility, November 2014.

LAUNCH FACILITY

Space X currently operates three orbital launch sites, at Cape Canaveral, Vandenberg Air Force Base, and Kennedy Space Center, and have announced plans for a fourth in Brownsville, Texas. Space X has indicated that they see a niche for each of the four orbital facilities and that they have sufficient launch business to fill each pad. Before it was retired, all Falcon 1 launches took place at the Ronald Reagan Ballistic Missile Defense Test Site on Omelek Island.
